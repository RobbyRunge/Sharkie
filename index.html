SHARKIE

Game Controls

Use the following controls to play Sharkie:

  • Arrow Keys: Move Sharkie in all directions
  • E: Give you a 50% Speed Boost of Movement for 5 Seconds, when you have a Coin
  • D: Throw poisoned Bubble, when you have a Poision Bottle
  • Space: Fin Slap Attack
